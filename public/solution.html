The Solution

An intelligent wearable that translates the physical world into audio context. A seamless fusion of computer vision, sensors, and cognitive AI.

Hands-Free Awareness

Designed as a non-intrusive headset, leaving hands completely free for a cane or other tasks.

Real-Time Processing

Low-latency spatial mapping ensures the user receives feedback within milliseconds of movement.

Cognitive Audio

Not just raw data—the device provides semantic descriptions (e.g., 'A chair is 2 meters ahead').

Edge Computing

Runs neural networks locally to maintain privacy and function without a stable internet connection.
