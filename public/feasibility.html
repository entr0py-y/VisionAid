Healthcare Impact

Six dimensions of measurable real-world impact on the lives of visually impaired individuals.

Autonomous Navigation

Enables fully independent movement without reliance on human guides or assistants. Users navigate public spaces, commute, and travel safely on their own terms.

Proactive Safety

Real-time hazard detection actively prevents accidents before they occur. Objects, obstacles, and dangers are identified and announced with enough lead time to react.

Increased Dignity

Reduces caregiver dependency and promotes equality. Independence restores dignity — users live on their own terms without constant reliance on others.

Mental Well-being

Reduces travel-related stress, fear, and anxiety. Knowing hazards are monitored continuously gives users confidence to move through the world without apprehension.

Enhanced Confidence

Empowers users to commute, shop, and engage in social activities independently. The system acts as a reliable invisible guide, expanding the user's world.

Quality of Life

Safety and technology-driven inclusive lifestyle. Vision AID directly raises the floor for what's possible — not just assistive, but transformative for daily living.

Risk & Mitigation

RISK Latency on RPi

→ Lightweight YOLO nano model; quantized inference; threaded pipeline to avoid blocking

RISK Limited Detection Accuracy

→ Custom YOLO fine-tuning on relevant object classes planned for Phase 02

RISK Hardware Constraints

→ Nano models + optimised pipelines; future upgrade path to RPi 5 or Coral TPU

RISK Cognitive Overload

→ Smart speech timing — priority-gated announcements prevent information saturation

RISK Scalability

→ Optional cloud layer (Phase 03) handles fleet management, model updates & analytics
